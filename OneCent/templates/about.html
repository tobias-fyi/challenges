Hi!
My name is Tobias.

---- ∫ ----

For those who don't know exactly what the #100DaysofX challenge is, it simply means that I'm dedicating myself to doing an activity or practicing a skill for at least 1 hour every day for 100 days in a row.

It all started when in March of 2019. I'd started learning how to develop software a few months before that and wanted to make learning and writing code a daily habit. I ran across the #100DaysofCode challenge and decided it would be a great way to make that happen. I finished th coding challenge in the middle of June, and started my writing one the next day. All 100 of the daily coding journals from #100DaysofCode can be found on my GitHub (along with the stories and the code for this site).

It was such so effective for coding that I decided to do the same thing with other skills that I've wanted to develop.

---- ∫ ----

I've been a sci-fi enthusiast and on-and-off writer since I was a little tyke. I've always enjoyed writing, but had never dedicated myself to developing the skill in any meaningful way...until now.

One reason I never spent any meaninful amount of time writing fiction is because I only ever did it when I had tons of inspiration. The whole point of the challenge is to make the promise to myself that I will write for at least an hour every single day, whether I feel like it, am inspired, have time, or not. No excuses...

Some of the stories are good; some are ok; some may be terrible or undeveloped or have any number of qualities that don't make me happy.

And that's ok—I'm going to write anyways.

It's all a practice.

---- ∫ ----

I hope you enjoy at least some of my stories. If you do, I'd love to hear about it! (Or if you have some constructive criticism).
Shoot me an email at hi@tobias.fyi or tweet @tobiasfyi.

Never stop exploring.
-Tobias
